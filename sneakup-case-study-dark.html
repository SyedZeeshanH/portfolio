SneakUp

Your Sneakers, Your Style

A premium sneaker shopping app that brings all major brands together in one seamless experience. Discover, browse, and buy the latest and most iconic sneakers.

Role
UI/UX Designer
Timeline
3 Weeks
Platform
Mobile (iOS/Android)
Tools
Figma

Project Overview

Sneaker culture has exploded in recent years, but finding authentic branded sneakers from multiple brands often requires visiting numerous websites and apps. SneakUp was designed to solve this fragmentation by creating a unified platform where sneaker enthusiasts can discover and purchase shoes from all major brands in one elegant experience.

The app targets sneakerheads, fashion-conscious shoppers, and young adults who want easy access to the latest releases and iconic classics without the hassle of jumping between different brand websites.

The Problem

Through market research and user interviews with sneaker enthusiasts, I identified key pain points in the current sneaker shopping experience:

  • Fragmented Shopping: Users have to visit multiple apps/websites to browse different brands
  • Missing Releases: It's easy to miss new drops when they're spread across different platforms
  • Comparison Difficulty: Hard to compare prices and styles across brands
  • Time Consuming: Shopping for sneakers takes too long with multiple apps
  • Authenticity Concerns: Users worry about buying fake sneakers from third-party sellers

Target Audience

Primary Users: Sneaker enthusiasts and collectors (ages 18-35) who actively follow sneaker culture and want access to the latest releases.

Secondary Users: Fashion-conscious shoppers who appreciate quality footwear and want a curated selection of premium brands.

User Goals: Find authentic branded sneakers easily, discover new releases, compare options across brands, and make purchases with confidence.

Design Process

1. Research & Discovery

I analyzed existing e-commerce apps including Nike SNKRS, Adidas, StockX, and GOAT to understand industry standards and identify opportunities for differentiation. Key insight: users wanted a simpler, more unified experience without losing brand identity.

2. User Personas & Journey Mapping

I created detailed personas representing different types of sneaker shoppers—from casual buyers to serious collectors. This helped inform features like brand filters, popular items showcase, and personalized recommendations.

3. Information Architecture

The app structure prioritizes discovery and quick purchasing:

  • Onboarding → Brand Discovery → Personalized Home Feed
  • Browse by Brand/Category → Product Details → Cart → Checkout
  • Wishlist → Favorites → Quick Reorder

4. Wireframing & Prototyping

Starting with low-fidelity wireframes, I explored different layout options and navigation patterns. The focus was on creating an intuitive browsing experience that felt premium yet accessible.

Design Decisions

Color Palette

The bold black and red color scheme was chosen to convey sophistication, energy, and premium quality:

Black #000000
Primary Background
Dark Gray #1a1a1a
Secondary
Coral Red #FF6B6B
Accent & CTAs
White #FFFFFF
Text & Cards

Why Dark Theme? Black backgrounds make product images pop and create a premium, gallery-like feel that's popular in sneaker culture. The red accent provides energy and draws attention to important actions.

Typography & Visual Hierarchy

Bold, modern typography with strong contrast ensures readability against dark backgrounds. Product names and prices are prominently displayed, while secondary information remains accessible but doesn't compete for attention.

Key Features & Screens

1. Brand-Focused Onboarding

The splash screens and onboarding immediately establish SneakUp's premium identity with the tagline "Your Sneakers, Your Style." The onboarding screen "Discover Your Style" sets expectations for a curated, personalized shopping experience.

2. Unified Brand Discovery

The home screen showcases all major brands (Nike, Adidas, Under Armour, New Balance) in one place with quick-access brand buttons. Featured promotions like "New User Deal - 20% OFF" drive immediate engagement.

3. Most Popular Section

Highlighting trending and popular items helps users discover what others are buying. Each product card includes essential info: image, name, price, and wishlist functionality—keeping the interface clean yet informative.

4. Detailed Product Pages

Product detail pages provide comprehensive information including multiple color options, size selection, pricing, and detailed descriptions. The "Add to cart" button uses the signature red accent to drive conversions.

5. Streamlined Shopping Cart

The cart interface shows all selected items with quantity controls, size display, and instant price calculations. The prominent "Checkout" button ensures a smooth path to purchase.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1

Problem: How to showcase multiple brands without losing individual brand identity

Solution 1

Created distinct brand filter buttons with authentic logos while maintaining a unified visual system through consistent dark theme and layout

Challenge 2

Problem: Making product images stand out in a dark interface

Solution 2

Used dark gray cards with subtle shadows that make sneaker images pop while maintaining visual hierarchy and premium feel

Challenge 3

Problem: Simplifying the checkout process for quick purchases

Solution 3

Designed a streamlined cart with all essential info visible and a prominent checkout button to reduce friction

Key Takeaways

  • Consolidation creates value: Bringing multiple brands together in one app solves a real pain point for sneaker shoppers
  • Dark UI for product showcase: Black backgrounds make product photography shine and create a premium aesthetic
  • Balance brand identity with unity: Respected individual brand logos while maintaining a cohesive experience
  • Speed matters in e-commerce: Quick filters, clear navigation, and streamlined checkout increase conversions

Impact & Results

SneakUp demonstrates my ability to design sophisticated e-commerce experiences that solve real market problems. The design balances premium aesthetics with functional shopping features.

  • Created a unified platform for multiple premium brands
  • Designed an intuitive browsing experience with quick brand filtering
  • Developed a premium dark theme that enhances product presentation
  • Built a streamlined cart and checkout flow for faster purchases
